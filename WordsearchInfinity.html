Wordsearch Infinity

Infinite cascading search grid. Find words, level up your board, beat the clock.

How to Play

  • Drag or touch-connect letters in any straight line (diagonals included!).
  • Minimum word size is 3 letters.
  • In Grow Mode, start with a 4x4 grid and expand up to 9x9 by scoring points!
  • Need a boost? Use the Hint button to reveal a letter for -100 pts.
